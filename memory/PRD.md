# PRD: WTF Agency Wine Proposal Landing Page

## Original Problem Statement
Create a professional proposal landing page for WTF Agency presenting comprehensive wine brand development services to Grupo Comercial Grupo Upper Blanc.
- Client: Grupo Comercial Grupo Upper Blanc
- Monthly Fee: USD 7,500
- Total Annual Investment: USD 90,000
- Structure: 2 development phases (3 months each) + 6 months growth management

## User Personas
- **Primary**: Business executives at Grupo Upper Blanc deciding on wine brand development partnership
- **Secondary**: Decision makers reviewing the proposal on mobile/desktop

## Core Requirements
- Hero section with both logos and pricing
- Interactive project phases (3 phases)
- Investment structure display
- WTF Methodology section
- 12-month timeline visualization
- Team credentials section
- Deliverables per phase
- Contact form (database storage only)
